# Images Folder

Place your images here. The template expects the following images:

## Banner Image
- `pic10.jpg` - Main banner/hero image

## Article Images
- `pic01.jpg` through `pic06.jpg` - Article card thumbnails

## Generic Page
- `pic11.jpg` - Featured image for generic page

## Recommended Sizes

- **Banner Image**: 600x400px or larger (maintains aspect ratio)
- **Article Thumbnails**: 400x250px (16:10 aspect ratio)
- **Featured Images**: 1200x600px or larger

## Free Image Resources

- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) - High quality free photos
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com) - Free images and videos

## Quick Start

You can download placeholder images from [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) or use services like:
- https://picsum.photos/600/400 (for random images)
- https://placeholder.com (for placeholder images)
